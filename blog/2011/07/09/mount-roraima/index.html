Mount Roraima

[caption id=”attachment_169” align=”aligncenter” width=”300” caption=”Mount Roraima - Venezuela”][/caption] [caption id=”attachment_170” align=”aligncenter” width=”300” caption=”Mount Roraima – Venezuela”][/caption] The highest tepui plateau (table-top surface) of the Pacaraima Mountains – Mount Roraima. It’s surrounded by 400 meter tall cliffs and most of the environment on the summit is rock. Despite minute resources such as soil, plants and animals THRIVE in this environment. Many of which, if not all, only exist on Mount Roraima. CHECK OUT THIS TOAD!!! This habitat is truly a “lost world.”
