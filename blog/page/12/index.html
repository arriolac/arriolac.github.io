The JOCC Blog

My friends and I are collaborating on this blog called The Life of a JOCC. Our original intention behind the blog is for us to have a place where we can share our experiences and lessons learned in life. It would be a blog wherein people can relate and share their experiences as well. Through time, we refined this idea and came up with the concept of a JOCC. A JOCC basically embodies the core values that my friends and I believe in. We strive to be JOCCs and working on this together is our very own way of expressing the JOCCs within ourselves. On our recent post, we address exactly “What is a JOCC?” Check it out right here!!

Photoshop Power

I’ve always been disappointed with the photos I take whenever I go travel. For instance, when I went on a roadtrip from NJ to CA the photos did not do the actual sights any justice. So I decided to save up some cash and purchase an SLR—I’m getting the Canon rebel t2i next week!!! However, as it turns out, photography is not all about the camera, it’s also about the post-modifications you make to your images. Anyway, I was messing around with photoshop lately and I really wanted to tweak this image I had of torrey pines in San Diego. It was a nice shot but it had poor lighting.
